Our plan

A plan that will allow people, anywhere in the world to transform plastic waste into products.

1. Develop Machines

For the past two years we have been developing machines to recycle plastic waste, locally.

2. Share, for free

The machines are developed using basic materials and we share the blueprints Open Source online. Making it possible for people all over the world to re-build them.

Show instruction videos

3. Spread the know-how

In order to build these machines people need to know the blueprints are available. We need to spread the know-how into every corner in the world.

Share the project

4. Create

Once the machines are build people can start experimenting, creating and producing new products from their local plastic waste.

Things you can create

5. Clean up

The primary goal is to recycle as much plastic as we possibly can. This would clean up our shared environment, improve living conditions and possibly create financial value!

6. Community

An important aspect of the project is to create a world wide community of like-minded plastic savers. People working for a cleaner future, sharing knowledge, helping each other and collaborating.

In case you didn't notice..

The world is submerged with plastic, from the heights of Himalayas to the Atlantic ocean we have tons of toxic plastic laying around.  And this plastic is here to stay, round about 450 years scientists say.

Whether we like it or not, plastic is our inherited toxic legacy. Let's use it as a resource to create new value and social innovation.
